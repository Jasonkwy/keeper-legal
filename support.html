Frequently Asked Questions

How does Keeper work?

You press "I'M SAFE" once a day to confirm you and your pet are okay. If you miss your check-in window, Keeper automatically sends an alert email (and SMS for Australian numbers) to your designated guardians.

What happens if I miss a check-in?

When your check-in window expires, Keeper sends an emergency alert to all your guardians. You will also receive warning notifications at 4 hours and 1 hour before your window closes.

How do I add or remove a guardian?

Tap "Guardians" on the main screen. You can add, edit, or remove guardian contacts at any time. Pro users can add up to 3 guardians; Free users can add 1.

SMS alerts are not being received — why?

SMS alerts are currently available for Australian mobile numbers only. If your guardian has a non-Australian number, they will still receive email alerts. International SMS support is planned for a future update.

How do I change the check-in window length?

Custom check-in window lengths (other than 24 hours) are a Pro feature. Tap "Settings" → "Check-In Window" to adjust. Upgrade to Pro to unlock this feature.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Keeper to cancel or change your plan at any time.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Keeper → Settings → tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the App from your device removes all locally stored data (check-in history, streak, settings). Guardian contact data stored on our server can be deleted by contacting us at support@stechbds.com.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

We'd love to hear from you. Email us at support@stechbds.com with a description of the issue or idea. Screenshots are always helpful!
