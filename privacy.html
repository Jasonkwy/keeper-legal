What We Collect

✓ Pet name (stored locally on your device, and synced to our secure backend so alerts can include it)
✓ Guardian names, email addresses, and phone numbers (stored securely in Supabase, used only to send alerts)
✓ Check-in timestamps (stored locally on your device, and synced to our secure backend so alerts can be sent even if the App isn't open)
✓ Subscription status (managed by RevenueCat)

✗ We do not collect your location
✗ We do not collect photos or media
✗ We do not sell or share your data with advertisers

How We Use Your Information

  • To send emergency email and SMS alerts to your designated guardians when a check-in window expires
  • To manage your Pro subscription and restore purchases
  • To maintain your check-in streak and habit history on your device

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat – subscription management (privacy policy)
  • Supabase – secure backend storage and alert dispatch (privacy policy)
  • Resend – email alert delivery (privacy policy)
  • Twilio – SMS alert delivery, available for Australian numbers only (privacy policy)

Data Retention

Guardian contact data is stored securely in Supabase and used only to send alerts. You may remove guardians at any time within the App. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Keeper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
